* **Salience Model**
	* Framework to classify stakeholders based on three key attributes : Power, Urgency and Legitimacy
	* This model helps identify and classify stakeholders according to their influence and needs
	* Ensures most critical stakeholders receive more attention and engagement
* **Stakeholder classes**
	* Stakeholders classified in to seven distinct categories:
		* Dormant - posses power but lack urgency and legitimacy
		* Discretionary - Have legitimacy but lack power and urgency
		* Demanding - Have urgency but lack power and legitimacy
		* Dominant - Have power and legitimacy but lack urgency
		* Dangerous - Have power and urgency but lack legitimacy
		* Dependent - Have urgency and legitimacy but lack power
		* Definitive - Have all three

* Power/Interest grid - Stakeholder cube (Influence vs Interest graph)
	* Low Power + Low Interest - Monitoring
	* Low power + High Ineterst - Keep informed (Encourage them to stay supportive without expending too much energy)
	* High power + Low interest - Keep satisfied
	* High power + High interest - Monitor closely
